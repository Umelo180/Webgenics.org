About Us

Webgenics is a Tech company known for quality Web designs and pages, we offer both Frontend & Backend services, we love to build Websites and other Tech projects for commercial and personal use. We create clean codes and and amazing designs to suit your choice. We offer services Nationwide and Internationally too, we are always available anytime, anyday to serve you. All you have to do is to contact us and talk business. We specialises on so many Programming Languages such as; HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, REACT, PHP, TAILWIND CSS, BOOTSTRAP e.t.c. Webgenics.Co was founded in the year 2023, precisely 24th February. The Founders of Webgenics.Co are Mr Beckham and Mr Emmanuel, they started gradually together as two individuals in Tech with no financial support. The days of their little beginning was rough, they encountered so many challenges, no one supported their ideas, no one patronises them but they kept on moving foward with the hope of getting Established one day which later came to pass. They started this very journey during their university days, just the two of them. tho it wasn't easy for them to balance their Tech life and Academic activities. but they did overcame those Challenges and Hindrances. Webgenics.Co headquaters is based at OWERRI, Nigeria.
